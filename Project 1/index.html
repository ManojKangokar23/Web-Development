About Us

Chikkamagaluru, nestled in Karnataka's Western Ghats, is a captivating destination known for its lush coffee plantations, misty hills, and serene landscapes. Often referred to as the "Coffee Land of Karnataka" it offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and culture. The region is home to Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka, which attracts trekkers and nature enthusiasts with its panoramic views. Coffee lovers can explore sprawling estates, learn about coffee cultivation, and enjoy freshly brewed coffee. Waterfalls like Hebbe Falls and Kalhatti Falls add to the region's charm, while ancient temples such as Amruteshvara and Kodandarama showcase its rich heritage. Wildlife enthusiasts can visit Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary to spot diverse flora and fauna. With its cool climate, scenic beauty, and opportunities for trekking, camping, and relaxation, Chikkamagaluru is an ideal getaway for travelers seeking tranquility and adventure.
